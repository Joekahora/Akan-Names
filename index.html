Hi and welcome to Akan Names

This web application requires you to input your date of birth calculating the day of the week you were born and select your gender from the form below then it will output your Akan Name. Akan Name is a naming system in ghana where the First Name is a "Day Name" meaning like Sunday is Kwasi, Monday is Kwadwo etc.
